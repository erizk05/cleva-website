Engineering the Future of Mobility

At CLEVA, we leverage cutting-edge technology to create electric vehicles that are efficient, reliable, and sustainable.

Advanced Battery Systems

Our vehicles utilize state-of-the-art Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO4) batteries, offering superior safety, extended lifespans, and reliable performance across diverse climates.

Smart Fleet Telematics

Integrated IoT sensors provide real-time data on vehicle health, location, and driver behavior, empowering fleet operators with actionable insights to optimize efficiency.

Lightweight Architecture

By employing advanced aerospace-grade materials, we minimize vehicle weight without compromising structural integrity, leading to extended range and lower energy consumption.

Regenerative Braking

Our intelligent braking system captures kinetic energy typically lost during deceleration and feeds it back into the battery, maximizing efficiency in stop-and-go transit routes.
